About

Robert is an experienced STEM tutor and Software Engineer with seven years of experience in mathematics and computer science tutoring. Passionate about education, Robert specializes in guiding students through complex concepts with clarity and enthusiasm.

With a background in research and development at NASA and MIT, Robert brings practical insights to his tutoring approach, offering real-world applications of academic subjects. Committed to empowering students to excel, Robert provides personalized support to help individuals achieve their educational goals.
